About Me

Greetings, I am Abdulrahman, a dedicated web developer with a focus on digital health advocacy. This project, titled DesignPulse: A Telemedicine UI/UX Feedback Portal, represents the culmination of my academic endeavors to create an intuitive and responsive platform that enhances user experience in healthcare technology. My expertise specialize in developing accessible telemedicine solutions that connect patients with healthcare providers seamlessly. My goal is to leverage technology to improve healthcare access, particularly through innovative platforms that facilitate communication and care delivery. Through this assignment, I aim to gather valuable feedback to further refine the design and functionality of telemedicine applications, ensuring they meet the needs of users.
